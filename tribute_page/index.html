Real Madrid Achievements

  • 1920 - The word real is Spanish for royal and was bestowed to the club by King Alfonso XIII, together with the royal crown in the emblem.

  • 1950-1960 - The club won the European Cup five times in a row. After these five consecutive successes, Real was permanently awarded the original cup and earning the right to wear the UEFA badge of honour

  • 2000 - Real Madrid was recognised as the FIFA Club of the 20th Century.

  • 2004 - Awarded the FIFA Centennial Order of Merit.

  • 2007 - Real Madrid was considered the most valuable football brand in Europe by BBDO.

  • 2010 - The club was also awarded Best European Club of the 20th Century by the IFFHS.

  • 2017 - The team succeeded in becoming the first club to win back to back Champions Leagues, then made it three in a row in next year, extending their lead atop the UEFA club rankings.
